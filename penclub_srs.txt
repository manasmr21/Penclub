PenClub – Software Requirements Specification
 (SRS)
 
1. Overview
PenClub is a digital platform designed to connect authors and readers in a single community.
The platform allows authors to showcase their work, publish blogs, promote books, and participate
in literary events,
while readers can discover books, follow authors, and share reviews. The system maintains
separate Author and Reader databases
with personalized feeds based on interests.
2. User Roles
Two primary roles exist in the system:
1. Author – Can publish content, promote books, and interact with readers.
2. Reader – Can explore books, follow authors, review books, and comment on posts.
3. Authentication System
Users can register and log in securely.
Author Registration Fields:
 Full Name
 Pen Name (optional)
 Email
 Password
 Phone Number
 Country / City
 Short Bio
 Genres / Interests
 Social Links (optional)
 Profile Picture
Reader Registration Fields:
 Full Name
 Email
 Password


--- PAGE 2 ---
 Interests / Genres
 Profile Picture (optional)
4. Interest-Based Feed
After registration users choose areas of interest such as Poetry, Fiction, Sci■Fi, Fantasy,
Romance, Thriller, Philosophy, or Self■help. The system generates a personalized feed containing
blogs, books, and events based on selected interests.
5. Author Features
Book Promotion:
Authors can list books with title, cover image, description, genre, release date, and purchase links.
Readers can review and comment on books.
Blog Publishing:
Authors can publish blogs discussing writing processes, literature insights, or book reviews.
Each blog includes title, content, tags, cover image, and publication date.
6. Reader Features
Readers can browse books, follow authors, comment on blogs, rate books, and write reviews.
Reviews include rating (1■5 stars), review text, and timestamp.
7. Notifications System
Users receive notifications for events, replies to comments, book announcements, author posts,
and invitations. Notifications appear in the in■app notification panel and optionally through email.
8. Core Database Entities
Main data collections include:
 Authors
 Readers
 Books
 Blogs
 Reviews
